How It Works
Group 1 Punters · Full season 2026/27
Virtual bankroll

Every race day with a Group 1 you get $100 virtual to bet. Allocate exactly $100 across all Group 1s that day.

Bet types

Win — must finish 1st
Place — must finish top 3
Each Way — half win, half place

Returns

All returns use TAB Starting Price (SP) after the race. Stake × SP odds.

Entry

$18.50 per week · ~$962 per player per year. Winner takes $1,000, 2nd takes $500. Rest goes to social fund.

Scratchings

If your horse is scratched, your bet is settled at the race favourite's SP (shortest win odds at start).

Social fund

All entry fees minus prizes go to one big end of season event for all couples.
