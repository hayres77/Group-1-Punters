How It Works
Group 1 Punters · Full season 2026/27 · All Australian Group 1s
Virtual bankroll

Every race day with a Group 1, you get $100 virtual to bet. You must allocate exactly $100 across all Group 1s that day — not a dollar more or less.

Bet types

Win — horse must finish 1st
Place — horse must finish top 3
Each Way — half stake win, half stake place

Returns

All returns use TAB Starting Price (SP) after the race. Win = stake × SP win odds. Place = stake × SP place odds.

Entry fee

$18.50 per week regardless of how many Group 1 race days that week. ~$962 per player per year.

Prizes

1st $1,000 · 2nd $500. Highest total virtual returns across the full season. All remaining pool goes to the social fund for the end of season event.

Social fund

All entry fees minus the $1,000 prize split equally across all couples at season end for one big group event.

Missing a race day: Score $0 for that day if no bets placed before first jump.
Tie-breaking: Most individual race day wins → most Group 1 winners backed.
